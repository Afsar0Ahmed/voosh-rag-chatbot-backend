Title: Salt leads England to first T20 win over Ireland
Link: https://www.bbc.com/sport/cricket/articles/cewn0kd7l54o?at_medium=RSS&at_campaign=rss
Published: Wed, 17 Sep 2025 17:11:53 GMT

Phil Salt again leads the way for England as they beat Ireland by four wickets in the first match of a three-game T20 series in Dublin.
